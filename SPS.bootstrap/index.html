Our Story

Just like that dedicated roommate who quietly keeps everything in order, our garden thrives through the love and effort of those who care for it.

Here, we believe that every person can be a part of something magical—transforming our botanical paradise into a vibrant sanctuary for all. Founded by a group of passionate nature enthusiasts, A Fairy Great Garden was born. Our garden serves not only as a haven for native plants and wildlife, but also as a gathering place for volunteers eager to contribute to something greater than themselves.

Garden Weeder

As a volunteer weeder at the botanical garden, you will help maintain the health and beauty of the garden by removing unwanted weeds from flower beds, pathways, and other landscaped areas. The role requires attention to detail and the ability to identify common weeds versus desired plants.

Seasonal Volunteer

As a volunteer seasonal worker at the botanical garden, you will assist with a variety of tasks that support the garden's needs throughout the year. You will help with tasks like mulching, pruning, and preparing garden beds for upcoming seasons, ensuring plants are healthy and well-maintained.

Entrance Booth

As a volunteer entry booth worker at the botanical garden, you will greet visitors, assist with ticket sales, and provide information about the garden’s exhibits, events, and programs. You will help ensure a smooth and welcoming entry experience, answering questions and directing guests to key areas of the garden.

Irrigation Manager

As a volunteer plant waterer at the botanical garden, you will be responsible for ensuring that plants across various garden areas receive adequate water, especially during dry periods. This includes watering plants, trees, and assisting with checking irrigation systems.


Explore

Welcome to the Fairy Great Garden, where the wonders of nature bloom in harmony with the magic of the fairy world. As you wander through our enchanted paths, you’ll be delighted by a variety of unique and beautiful plants, each cared for by our fairy gardeners. Every flower and plant tells a story, from rare blossoms that glow softly at dusk to vines that whisper in the breeze. Here are just a few of the extraordinary species you can expect to encounter on your journey


  • Moonlit Orchids

    Delicate, shimmering blooms that glow under the moon's light. These ethereal orchids are the pride of our garden. Known for their soft, luminous petals, the Moonlit Orchid blooms only when the moon is high in the sky, casting a silvery glow across the garden. It is said that these flowers are the favorite of moonbeam fairies, who dance among the petals each night.

  • Fairy Bellflowers

    Tiny, bell-shaped flowers that ring when the wind blows. These enchanting bellflowers come in shades of lavender, pink, and pale blue. Their petals are delicate and translucent, with a soft chime that resonates when the wind plays through the garden. Fairy Bellflowers are known to be favorites among the pixies, who often hide their tiny wings under their blooms for a quick rest.

  • Sparkling Moss

    Lush, glowing moss that carpets the garden’s stone paths. A soft carpet of moss, known for its sparkling, dewy texture, lines the pathways of the Fairy Great Garden. The moss is a blend of green and gold, and in the early morning light, it gives off a faint glitter that is said to attract wandering forest sprites. If you step carefully, you might just find a hidden entrance to one of the fairy homes tucked beneath the mossy surface.
